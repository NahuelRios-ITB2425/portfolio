Project 1: BigoTech

This project focused on the implementation of a robust technological infrastructure for a new company. A scalable architecture was developed using a LAMP stack (Linux, Apache, MySQL, PHP) and special emphasis was placed on cybersecurity, the protection of digital assets and the creation of a business portal that reflected the company's values. Throughout the process, the team overcame several challenges related to talent recruitment and project fluctuations, but reached important milestones, such as the successful implementation of the stack and portal. At the end of the project, the team's learning and ability to adapt were highlighted, with a strong focus on continuous improvement and excellence.

Our team worked on creating a scalable architecture to support future growth, and our primary focus is network security, scalability, and high efficiency.

Project 2: SmartWaste

The SmartWaste project focuses on automating waste management using advanced technologies such as computer vision and artificial intelligence. Use devices like the Raspberry Pi, equipped with cameras and specialized software to recognize and classify waste types, such as plastic, paper, glass and metal. This system helps improve the efficiency of recycling and waste separation, reducing pollution and promoting the circular economy. In addition, it allows real-time data to be collected on the composition and flow of waste, which facilitates informed decision-making for authorities and organizations responsible for waste management.

Our solution focuses on environmental sustainability while improving operational efficiency, and reducing costs for municipalities and corporations alike. In short, SmartWaste is an innovative solution to optimize waste management and make it more sustainable, while BigoTech focused on creating a technological business infrastructure, with a strong emphasis on security and scalability.
